Why should I care?




Learning about ocean pollution is important because it is a critical global issue that affects the health of the planet and all of us that live on it. By understanding the causes and consequences of ocean pollution, we can make informed decisions about how to protect the ocean and the life it supports. Knowledge about ocean pollution can also inspire us to take action and make changes in our own lives to reduce our impact on the ocean. The ocean is a significant part part of our lives since it is a prime source of food, economic activity, medicine, and many other things. Learning about ocean pollution motivates us to be part of the solution. By understanding the issues that affect our oceans, we can play a role in creating a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable ocean for future lives.




Others making a difference

Here are just some of the many organizations that do their part in protecting our oceans

The Ocean Cleanup

The Ocean Cleanup is a non-profit organization that is tackling ocean plastic pollution through advanced technology and active cleanup efforts. The organization is dedicated to collecting plastic waste from the ocean and preventing it from reaching our shores and harming marine life. With a focus on both cleanup and raising awareness, The Ocean Cleanup is working to create a cleaner and more sustainable ocean for future generations.







4Ocean

4ocean is a for-profit social enterprise that aims to reduce ocean plastic pollution by cleaning up the ocean and producing products made from recycled ocean plastic. The company also supports ocean conservation efforts through its 4ocean Foundation. By removing plastic waste from the ocean and creating sustainable products, 4ocean is working to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable ocean.

Plastics Ocean International

Plastic Oceans International is a non-profit dedicated to reducing ocean plastic pollution through education, research, and advocacy. They aim to promote sustainable practices, raise public awareness and create a cleaner ocean for future generations. By addressing the issue of plastic pollution in the ocean, Plastic Oceans International is working to protect our oceans and the diverse array of species that call them home.







How you can make a difference!

Here are some things you can change in your daily lives to ensure the ocean will not continue to be filled by pollution.




  • Use less water
  • Less single use plastics
  • Organize trash more efficiently
  • Support ocean friendly products
  • Reduce Water Usage
